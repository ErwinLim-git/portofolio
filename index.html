Financial & Disbursement Automation

Checker-Maker Workflow
Problem Statement

High risk of double payments, manual reconciliation errors, and lack of proper audit trail in net-off disbursements.

Solution & Impact

Implemented double-layer approval (Maker/Checker) with full audit trail. Prevented double payments and completely eliminated manual errors.

Unique Amount Transfer (3-digit identifier)
Problem Statement

High transaction fees from Virtual Accounts and frequent matching failures during reconciliation.

Solution & Impact

Replaced expensive Virtual Accounts with standard bank transfers using auto-incremented 3-digit identifier for automatic matching. Saved ~Rp 30M per month.

Net Off Report + Bukti Potong (Tax) Automation
Problem Statement

Finance team spent excessive time on complex manual calculations and preparing tax withholding receipts.

Solution & Impact

Fully automated complex COD vs shipping fee calculations and daily automated tax receipt distribution. Created 100% system-generated single source of truth.

Automated COD Payouts & VA Status Scheduler
Problem Statement

Slow and error-prone manual bank transfers to sellers plus missed Virtual Account status updates.

Solution & Impact

Daily automated payouts combined with scheduled VA status reconciliation.
